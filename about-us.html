About Us

We are committed to providing  the best care solutions

White Glove Consulting is a Medicaid consulting firm that guides families and seniors through the complex process of applying for Medicaid. Whether you own a home, have a substantial amount of money in resources, or your monthly income is above the Medicaid allowable, here at White Glove Consulting we can assist anybody and everybody in becoming eligible for Community Medicaid.

Our Value

We put you first

24 Hour Care

Customer service is our top priority. We handle each case as if it's our only one, and we do anything possible to make the process easier on our clients.

Work with partners

We do plenty of consulting work for nursing homes, certified and licensed home care agencies, hospitals, assisted living facilities, etc. Most people that are referred to us are not eligible for Medicaid and therefore we work with them to make them eligible.

Professional Experts

Because of our expertise, we are not limited to helping just families but are also recognized as professional Medicaid consultants throughout the entire Health Care industry.

unique value

Pending applications

One of the best things we can offer is that the certified home health agencies accept our Medicaid pending applications and therefore can offer the unique possibility for home care service to begin, while the Medicaid approval is still pending. Once the Medicaid gets approved, the agency can back bill Medicaid for the pending services that were provided.
